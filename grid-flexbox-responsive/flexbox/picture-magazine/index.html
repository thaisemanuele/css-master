JANE & OLIVE

About us

Founded by the dynamic duo of visionary vanguards, Jane Sterling and Olive Monroe, our magazine celebrates the symphony of life's moments through the art of photography.

Nestled in the heart of the concrete jungle, New York City, our magazine is a testament to the limitless power of female entrepreneurship. With a shared passion for capturing the ethereal essence of existence, Jane and Olive embarked on this cosmic journey armed with their cameras and an unquenchable thirst for innovation.

This magazine isn't just a collection of photographs; it's a gateway to uncharted dimensions, an ode to femininity's strength, and a kaleidoscope of inspiration for anyone who dares to dream.

The idea: A Vision Unveiled

Jane and Olive, both accomplished photographers in their own right, found themselves drawn together by a shared yearning – a yearning to break the conventional barriers of photography and capture the intangible essence of life. They were tired of fleeting snapshots; they craved the stories that danced within each image, waiting to be whispered to the world.

The turning point came during a photography exhibition, where their individual works were displayed side by side. As they observed visitors lost in the narratives their photographs wove, a spark ignited. It was an epiphany – a call to transcend their individual prowess and embark on a joint odyssey. Their vision was to create a sanctuary within the pages of a magazine, where visual stories could breathe and transcend the ordinary.

Jane and Olivia spent months meticulously curating their first issue, infusing it with the essence of their collective spirit. The layout, the choice of images, the poetry that adorned the pages – every element was a testament to their unwavering dedication. This magazine emerged as a tribute to the power of unity, creativity, and womanhood.
